### Overview
- Led the end-to-end build of a streaming analytics platform that ingests 30+ data feeds and surfaces explainable risk signals for trading teams.
- Partnered with Quant Research to calibrate model outputs to Value-at-Risk and stress metrics used in daily governance.
- Rolled out to fixed income, equities, and commodities desks with 99.95% availability and automated escalation playbooks.

### Technology Highlights
- Distributed time-series transformers and anomaly detection tuned for market microstructure.
- Self-service dashboards with provenance tracing, enabling teams to drill from signal to raw evidence.
- Cloud-native architecture hardened for cyber controls and regional data residency requirements.
