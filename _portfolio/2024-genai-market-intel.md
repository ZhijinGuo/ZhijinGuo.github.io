### Overview
- Architected a compliant retrieval-augmented generation workflow that surfaces trusted macro research, pricing commentary, and risk memos in seconds.
- Designed alignment guardrails with Legal, Compliance, and Model Risk to keep generated insights auditable.
- Drove a 4x reduction in time-to-answer for sales and research requests across North America and EMEA desks.

### Technology Highlights
- Enterprise knowledge graph + vector search to stitch market signals, analyst commentary, and regulatory constraints.
- Fine-tuned large language models on curated JPMorgan corpora with human-in-the-loop evaluations.
- Real-time usage analytics and feedback loops that continuously improve retrieval quality and prompt strategy.
